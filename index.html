Koray Cosguner

Associate Professor of Marketing
John Rau Kelley School of Business Faculty Fellow

Koray Cosguner constitutes a bridge between marketing, economics, and psychology, employing rigorous quantitative methods to decode complex consumer decision-making processes. His research translates behavioral insights into actionable firm strategies, optimizing pricing architectures and non-pricing instruments across product, promotion, and placement.

Specializing in structural modeling, reduced form estimation, and causal inference, his scholarship addresses critical business challenges—from dynamic pricing algorithms to decision support systems for email marketing. His work appears in the discipline's premier outlets, including Marketing Science, Journal of Marketing Research, Management Science, and Journal of Retailing.
